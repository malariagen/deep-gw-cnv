Experiment 07 results summary:
- CRT: FNR=0.00, PPV=0.31, 118 FPs (was 142) — FPs reduced but still low PPV
- PM2_PM3: FNR=0.26 (was 0.20), PPV=0.36, 118 FPs (was 137) — FNR rose as predicted

- GCH1: FNR=0.17, PPV=0.83, 318 FPs — unchanged (CRR fallback, no HMM involvement)

My diagnosis:
Raising self_transition 0.75→0.80 worked directionally but modestly. Borderline FPs
(CRR p50 ~1.20–1.21) survived because they sit just below the TP floor — CRT TPs
start at CRR p10=1.50, PM2_PM3 TPs at p10=1.31 — and the transition penalty alone
can't cleanly separate that 0.13 gap between FP p50 and FN p50. PM2_PM3 FNR already
rose from 0.20 to 0.26 at 0.80, so the self_transition lever is effectively exhausted.
Population breakdowns show CRT FPs concentrated in AF-E (PPV=0.03) and PM2_PM3 FPs
in AF-W (PPV=0.04) — both consistent with genuine signal the GATK ground truth misses.

Proposed experiment 08: Raise cnv_min_confidence (0.50 → 0.65)
Changes from 07:
- cnv_min_confidence: 0.50 → 0.65
  The HMM's probability mass in the amplified state at the time of calling is low for
  borderline samples (CRR ~1.20) and high for clear TPs (CRR ~1.64–1.88). This gate
  suppresses uncertain calls without touching the HMM transition dynamics that now
  bound PM2_PM3 FNR. Independent of self_transition; no re-segmentation needed.

What I expect:
CRT and PM2_PM3 FP counts should fall further. TPs at CRR 1.64–1.88 should be called
with high confidence and pass the gate. Main risk is PM2_PM3: FNs at CRR p50=1.33
may be called with low confidence — if they cluster just above 0.50, raising the gate
to 0.65 would push FNR up from 0.26. GCH1 uses the CRR fallback and is unaffected.

What we could do instead:
1. Raise confidence to 0.70 and combine with self_transition 0.80 — more aggressive,
   riskier for PM2_PM3 FNR.
2. Revert self_transition to 0.77 and rely on confidence filtering instead — if PM2_PM3
   FNR at 0.26 is already too high, a softer transition with tighter confidence may
   recover recall while still cutting FPs.

Run time estimate: No VAE retraining, no HMM re-segmentation. CNV calling + eval ~2 min.
